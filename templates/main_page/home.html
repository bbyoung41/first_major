WHY WRITE A LETTER

Writing to your future self is a proven method to boost self-awareness and long-term motivation. By setting clear goals and intentions today, you create a personal commitment that encourages accountability and reflection. Studies show that people who regularly document their aspirations are more likely to stay focused on self-improvement and achieve their goals. This simple act of writing helps bridge the gap between your present and future self, reminding you of the progress you've made and the dreams you're working toward.
